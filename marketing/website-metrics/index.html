Shipping & Fulfillment

Shipping Loss and Sales − Shipping Loss are calculated automatically from Collected/Cost/Sales — they're never entered directly, so they can't drift out of sync.

Where do these numbers come from?
Go to Insights > Reports > Shipping Cost (and Order Detail for Order Sales). Select SFCC Production, set the date range, then Export to CSV. In the export, sum the Order # column for Orders Shipped, Shipping Paid for Shipping Collected, Shipping Cost for Shipping Cost, Insurance Cost, Weight Shipped, and Items.
